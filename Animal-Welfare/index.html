PAST INVOLVEMENT

The Animal Welfare and The Anti-Rabies Act of the Philippines



This law's purpose is to help,protect, and treat all the animals in our country. In accomplience to this law, me and my dog traveled to our veterinarian for my dog's first anti rabies vaccine. Giving your dog an anti rabies vaccination will prevent you, and your dog having a fatal disease called rabies. Vaccination of your dog is one of many simple actions that we can do to promote and help animal welfare.

CURRENT INVOLVEMENT

SPECIAL DIET



This type of diet can help reduce the need of animals being used as human foods. It is said that reducing your animal products intake for one month can save 25kg of meat or equivalent to 2-3 chickens. Therefore, as a person who cares for animals I decided that I will only eat animal products 2-3 days a week which can contribute to reducing the need of animals being killed for human foods.

CURRENT INVOLVEMENT

Sharing videos and post in social media about animal welfare



Social media helps people to easily get connected to each other. As a person who cares for the well being of animals, this gives me a chance to raise the voice of animals for their well being using social media. By just sharing videos and posting online about animal welfare, I may be able to influence some of my friends in helping and protecting animals.

FUTURE INVOLVEMENT

As a future IT professional and a person who cares for animals, someday if I am able to get a future IT related job or business, I will donate 15% of my every salary to the organization that supports,protects,and helps all the animals.
